Education

Primary school started at Tshogoenpa Primary School, it was my first level of formal education my life. It hold such nostalgic memories of early education.

Wamrong Lower Secondary School was where I embarked on my first time broader educated journey for the first time. I studied there for two years 7 till 8. It build a strong educational base for the higher grades, setting the stage for their future academic journey .

Tashitse Higher Secondary School was perched at top of the hill, making it an unforgettable chapter in my life, where I had the chance to embark on new experiences. Sharing my studies made it a truly memorable journey.

This is a place where I can start a new chapter in my life(Samtse College of Education), and I am genuinely excited that one day I will have the opportunity to educate and inspire my future students.
